About

I'm a 21-year-old student pursuing a degree in Information Technology at National University. My passion for technology started early, and it's been fascinating to explore the various ways it has impacted our lives. At NU, I'm constantly challenged and inspired by my professors and peers, and I'm especially interested in learning more about software development

In my IT journey, I have been immersing myself in various programming languages, software development, networking, and cybersecurity. I have a strong desire to understand the intricacies of IT systems and contribute to advancing technology. I constantly seek new challenges and opportunities to apply my knowledge and skills in real-world scenarios. My goal is to make a meaningful impact in the field of information technology and to continue growing as a professional.

Beyond academics, I embrace a diverse set of hobbies that allow me to explore different aspects of life. When I need a mental break from the complexities of coding, I find solace in the immersive worlds of video games. Whether it's strategizing with teammates or tackling challenging solo quests, gaming allows me to unwind, exercise my problem-solving skills, and connect with friends online or in person.
